Sophie Spence

Sophie Spence is a Naarm based artist, currently undertaking a Bachelor of Arts (Photography) at RMIT. Her developing photographic practice explores personal experiences of womanhood and mental health. As a member of Collider she explores the use of archival material to consider and undertake a process of unlearning around social histories and sense of place.

You can find her at @sspences, or email her at spence.sophie@gmail.com
